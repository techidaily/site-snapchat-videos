![snapchat record](https://images.wondershare.com/filmora/article-images/snapchat-record.JPG)

3. You‚Äôll now notice that your iPhone‚Äôs screen has been mirrored with your Mac computer.
4. Now, on your iPhone, go to Snapchat and browse the inbox. Subsequently, on your Mac, tap the ‚ÄòRecord‚Äô button and then start viewing the snaps on your iPhone. QuickTime will record everything from the snaps and save it on your computer.
5. After you‚Äôre done with recording, tap the ‚ÄòRecord‚Äô button again to stop it. Lastly, go to ‚ÄòFile‚Äô and click ‚ÄòSave‚Äô, the file format to which the file will be saved is .mov which is generally supported by majority of movie players.
